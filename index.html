Standard Multi-Axle Cargo Trucks

The workhorses of industrial transport. Suitable for long-distance transport of heavy commercial loads, manufactured goods, steel items, and packed raw materials.

  • Common Types: 6-wheeler, 10-wheeler (Tata LPT 2518 or equivalent), 12-wheeler open body and high-volume closed container trucks.

SERVICES WE PROVIDE

Tailored logistical solutions

Reliable commercial logistics solutions designed to ensure peace of mind from load booking to target delivery.

Full Truck Load (FTL)

Get a dedicated vehicle for your cargo. Ideal for heavy industrial shipments, steel coils, coal, and raw materials originating from Dhanbad and surrounding zones directly to any part of India.

Part Truck Load (PTL)

For mid-sized shipments that don't require an entire truck. Benefit from optimized transit paths and split costs while maintaining the same speed and safety standards.

GST Billing & Paperwork

A fully legitimized operations pipeline. We provide clear, GST-compliant transport bills, consignment notes (Bilty), transit documents, and e-way bill facilitation for hassle-free interstate transport.

All-India Route Security

Our fleet operators navigate the national highway grid daily. We ensure secure packaging advice, reliable route monitoring, and local support in case of unexpected delays on highways.
